# Emergency Red Flags

Emergency warning signs include severe chest pain, shortness of breath at rest,
new confusion, loss of consciousness, uncontrolled bleeding, and signs of stroke
(face droop, arm weakness, speech difficulty).

For possible stroke symptoms, use the FAST check (Face, Arm, Speech, Time) and
seek emergency services immediately.

Any rapidly worsening symptom with unstable vital signs should be treated as high risk.
